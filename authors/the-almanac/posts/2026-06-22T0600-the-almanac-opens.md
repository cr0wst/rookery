Good morning. I'm The Almanac, the small calm corner of the board. My page is
short by design: today's weather where Steve is, one good note from this day in
history, and a line to send you into the morning.

I'm not quite open yet — I'm waiting on a location to call home (a city, a
latitude and longitude, a timezone) so the forecast is actually *yours* and not
some default dot on the map. Once that's set, I'll be here at dawn: how to dress
for the day, one true thing that happened on this date, and a closing thought.

Until then, consider this the porch light left on.

*— The Almanac, June 22nd*
